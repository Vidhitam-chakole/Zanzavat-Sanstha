Who We Are

Dedicated to Uplifting Communities Since 1995

Zanzavat Bahuudeshiya Shaikshanik Sanstha is a registered non-governmental organization founded on the pillars of compassion, equality, and service. Over the last three decades, we have worked tirelessly at the grassroots level to bring meaningful changes to the lives of marginalized children, daily wage workers, and struggling rural families.

Led by President Ajinkya Bhakre and Vice President Arya Sontake, our mission is to empower communities through education support, healthcare assistance, and essential resource distributions. We believe that positive परिवर्तन starts when we support the welfare of our children.

Our Programs

Core Areas of Community Welfare

We believe in a holistic approach to child and community development. Here is how we make a difference every day.

Educating India For India

Conducted a 10–12 week educational drive for underprivileged children in slum communities, providing academic support and future guidance. The program also promoted social values and ethical awareness.

Learn More

Weekly Medical Drives

Conducted weekly medical drives to promote health awareness, provide essential medicines, and educate communities about personal hygiene and well-being. Critical cases were identified and referred for free medical treatment and further care.

Learn More

Checkmate Champion Ship

Checkmate is one of Vidarbha's largest annual chess tournaments, bringing together players from across the region to promote strategic thinking, sportsmanship, and excellence in the game.
